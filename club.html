What the Club is

Robofront Club is the closed layer beneath the public desk. Where the briefings and field reports cover what is happening, the Club is where the desk says what it actually thinks — the implications for work, security, capital and society that don’t belong in a public note.

It is deliberately small. The value of an inner ring is that it stays one.
