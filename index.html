THE MARKET GAP

The robotics market is moving faster than its intelligence layer.

Robotics has become a hardware-defined market with a software-defined future. Capital, corporates and media can see the surface: humanoids, robot dogs, factory demos, launch videos. What remains difficult is the layer underneath: who is real, who is inflated, which supply chains matter, where deployments are happening, and how China’s ecosystem should be read in its own language.
