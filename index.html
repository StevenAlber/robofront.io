Independence & method

Independent by design. Verified by presence.

Robofront sells intelligence — not promotion. The methodology is built so that what you read is what was actually seen.

01 · Presence

Primary access, on the ground

Reporting begins in the field: factories, stores, deployment sites and markets across the Pearl River Delta — documented first-hand.

02 · Verification

Claims are tested, not repeated

Manufacturer statements are checked against what is visible, purchasable and operating. If it cannot be verified, it is labelled as unverified.

03 · Independence

No pay-for-coverage. Ever.

No company can buy a positive mention. Commercial relationships, where they exist, are disclosed. The reader's interest comes first.

04 · Synthesis

Multiple independent research streams

Field observation is cross-checked against independent research streams and primary documentation before anything is published.
